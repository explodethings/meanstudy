Meme Compatible

There was once a powerful meme, capable of eradicating the planet. Unfortunately, the zoomers have fought the boomers in an endless war and Mr. Rogers was killed, taking the meme with him. Today, powerful force submit their resources to find and become, MEME COMPATIBLE.

Meme Compatible 2

It took some time, but Microsoft had created a machine capable of generating such a timeless centerpiece of power. It required unimaginable resources, among them a horde of bongo cats, a single pepe (very rare these days) and several snitches. Not an easy task, for sure.

Meme Compatible 3

Eventually, from the dark reaches of the abyss, formed the second Meme Compatible. Amazed by this discovery, Microsoft promptly began putting up the raw power of auction. The auction was not to be for monetary value, but for more resources.
